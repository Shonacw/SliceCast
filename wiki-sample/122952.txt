========,1,preface.
Morehouse is a city in New Madrid County, Missouri, United States.
The population was 973 at the 2010 census.
========,2,History.
A post office called Morehouse has been in operation since 1888.
The community has the name of Albert P. Morehouse, the 26th Governor of Missouri from 1887 to 1889.
