========,1,preface.
Glade is a city in Phillips County, Kansas, United States.
As of the 2010 census, the city population was 96.
========,2,History.
Glade was a shipping point on the Atchison & Lenora division of the Missouri Pacific railroad.
A post office was opened in Glade in 1908, and remained in operation until 1989.
It was officially discontinued in 1996.
