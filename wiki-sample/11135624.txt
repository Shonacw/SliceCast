========,1,preface.
Başka 33/3 ("Different") is Işın Karaca's third solo album.
"Başka 33/3" was released on 19 June 2006.
In this album, she has written "Herşeye Rağmen" with Erdem Yörük.
The name of the album on the cover is stylized as "Başka" to highlight the word "aşk", which means "love" in Turkish.
The first video for the album is shot for "Mandalinalar", difrected by Kubilay Kasap.
The second video came to "Kalp Tanrıya Emanet".
Third video is an Onur Mete song "Bırakma".
