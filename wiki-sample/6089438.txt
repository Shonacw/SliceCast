========,1,preface.
Free at Last is the second studio album by rapper Freeway.
It was released on November 20, 2007 in the United States.
The album features production from J. R. Rotem, Needlz, and Cool & Dre, among others.
Guest appearances include Jay-Z, 50 Cent, Jadakiss and other notable rappers.

========,2,Sales.
"Free at Last" debuted at number 42 on the U.S. "Billboard" 200 chart, selling about 36,000 units during its first week.
As of April 2, 2008, the album has sold 100,206 copies in the United States.
